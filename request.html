Eligibility Criteria

  • Housing Status

    Currently experiencing homelessness, transitioning to permanent housing, or living in temporary/emergency shelter.

  • Financial Need

    Household income below local area median income or experiencing financial hardship due to job loss, medical emergency, or natural disaster.

  • Location

    Residing within our service area (approximately 50km radius from our distribution center).

  • Documentation

    Able to provide basic identification and proof of need (referral letter, social services documentation, etc.).
